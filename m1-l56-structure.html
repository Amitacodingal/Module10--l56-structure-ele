Healthy diet

A healthy diet plan includes a variety of foods from different food groups, and limits unhealthy foods. It also includes regular physical activity and staying hydrated


    List of healthy Foods to include in your diet :

  • Fruits and vegetables: Eat at least five portions of fruits and vegetables a day.
  • Whole grains: Choose whole grains like brown rice, oats, and wheat.
  • Protein: Eat protein-rich foods like meat, fish, eggs, beans, nuts, and legumes.
  • Healthy fats: Choose unsaturated oils and spreads. Dairy or dairy alternatives: Eat dairy or dairy alternatives like soya drinks.
